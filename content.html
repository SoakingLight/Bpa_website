WHY SHOULD YOU WORK FOR US?

At Car Little's Used Car Lot, we aim to provide for our employees as much as they provide for us. 70% of our interns are offered full time positions after their internship. Our Sales representatives make over $100,000 a year in salary, and up to half that in comissions. Car Little's Used Car Lot also offers benefits to all of our employees, including health insurance, car insurance, and even dental.
